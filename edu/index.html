Course objectives

  • teaching the tradition of the European Image
  • analysing and criticizing modern society through art
  • helping students to produce artworks according to traditional quality standards. (maybe not a problem in Russia but here for sure!)
  • individual orientation and criticism of the student s works

Inscription

Valid for a 6 month term Participants / students benefit from
  • 1-4 lectures/ month on chosen art problems by text with key pics for explanation
  • 2x personal assessment: can be answering students question or assessing his works.
  • 1x briefing per term in Prague (cheap transport and accommodation) which could end up in a small workshop.
Acknowledgment of successful participation of the term can be delivered online.

Target group

Students to be/ in preparation to enter fine art schools, students of fine arts, art amateurs, artists who wish communication, others ..
